About The Dataset

This Dataset is about cars from back in 1985.
It is a mix of continuous, ordinal and nominal variables,
which are all used to predict the price of the car.

There are 205 observations, with 26 attributes
including the target variable.

The dataset consists of 3 types of entities:
  1. the specification of an auto
  2. assigned insurance risk rating
  3. normalized losses compared to other cars
